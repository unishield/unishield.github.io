Welcome to UNISHIELD

What is the problem?

Scammers are Bad.
Scammers don’t value crypto but convert to FIAT.
Scammers are organized and their effort is constant.

One of the reasons why crypto and decentralization are not widespread is because of scams surrounding it.
People rather enjoy the “security” centralization gives them.

It is time to organize and fight back.

What do we want to do?

UniShield is a community of crypto enthusiasts who want to bring scams to a minimum.
We will build tools, provide education, and spread information.

How do we achieve that?

- We will have DAO and governance token
- We will be funded by our token treasury and donations
- We will give value to the community
- We will extract value to fund building more value
- We will use decentralized solutions like https://gitcoin.co/
